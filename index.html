Evolution, Growth and Progress

The journey of BIG Logistics began in the year 2012 and since then the growth has been steady. The company began from a rented space in Delhi, India with a staff 4.

From 2012 to 2017 they have grown to fully functional and owned infrastructures in 4 major Indian cities with Head Office in Delhi and branches in Mumbai, Chennai and Bengaluru.

The strength of the core staff also grew to 22. Each member at BIG Logistics is a professional belonging to the exhibition industry and is very well experienced to understand the needs of the clients and also knows the importance and usage of various tools required by the client to do their business without any hassles.

Safety of the clients’ is of major importance as told by the employees. Apart from fulfilling and meeting the demands of client employee satisfaction is also well taken care of.

Employees indulge in various trainings from time to time to keep themselves abreast with the latest news and technologies in event industry.

BIG Logistics is also an active member of IAEE, IEIA and AFECA. To provide their clients with the best and latest services the company makes sure that their employees attend various educational courses, like the CEM (Certified in Exhibition Management), and social seminars organized by various platforms like exhibition showcase, annual IEIA meeting, etc.

CEO, Mr. Uttam Gupta, takes special care that every employee who joins the organization shares the vision, mission and values upon which the company is built.

Such policies, openness in culture and the rich experience of the staff of the company has led to the steady growth, both in the number of clients and their emotional satisfaction with BIG Logistics India.

From the CEO's Desk

In my experience of more than 25 years in this industry, I have had a few learnings of my own.
We are living in an age of “VUCA times”. VUCA generally stands for – Volatility, uncertainty, complexity and ambiguity. However, due to the dynamic nature of world which we are living in, the new VUCA should be – Vulnerability, ulterior, contradictory and abnegation:
* Vulnerability – One cannot reduce vulnerability, a smart company is the one which can make plans to make themselves less vulnerable to external and unpredictable factors
* Ulteriority – To survive in this ultra-competitive environment we need to see the bigger picture. Everything is a subset of some master set, and hence the master set should shape our actions
* Contradictory – Every innovative idea invites contradictions. We need to have the reasoning and the planning to tackle the contradiction
* Abnegation – Self-denial is the biggest liability. As it is said, to win over one-self is the biggest victory, we need to convince ourselves of our vision and mission before our team and clients.

We, at BIG Logistics, are committed towards making the world a less vulnerable place for our clients and to keep them safe from these VUCA times, to exceed their expectations, meet deadlines and increase Economies of Scale thereby increasing profitability for all members of supply chain.

- Uttam Gupta (CEO)

Our Vision

The vision of BIG Logistics, as in the words of their CEO Mr. Uttam Gupta, is –

“Connecting all corners of the world through a diversified network of our services”

Events, fairs and exhibitions are the best way to display various technical, defense, cultural and other kinds of advancements made in one part of the world into another. The pace at which some countries progress is not the same as the rest. To bring all nations together and to provide everyone with the latest advancements, Exhibitions are the best way!

To make these exhibitions a seamless experience for the show organizers and the general public the job of BIG Logistics’ is to develop a well-knit, one-stop and reliable network across the world by means of Land, Air and Sea at the best competitive rates. Values of BIG Logistics
